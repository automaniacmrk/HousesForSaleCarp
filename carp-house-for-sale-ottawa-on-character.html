Carp House For Sale, Ottawa, On Character

The housing markets in Carp are mostly made up of single detached homes, with about three quarters being classified as such. One quarter of the remaining housing stock consists of townhouses built after 2000, when Carp experienced its greatest building boom. A small percentage (less than 10%), of row homes can also be found in this area. These houses have four bedrooms, but not more than five.

Amazing Surroundings

Carp Lake, a small lake located in Pierce County Washington. In every direction, there are bodies of water equal to or larger than the lake. To the north are a few lake visitors: the much larger Steilacoom Lake, Lake Louise, and Waughop Lake.

Two large lakes lie to the south: American Lake, and Gravelly Lake. Carp Lake lies east of the borders of two large water bodies: Nisqually Reach, and Carr Inlet.
